Inheritance Paths

Inheritance Paths are cooperative journeys that turn wisdom into lived practice. Each path spans several weeks, blends learning with embodiment, and invites community accountability without any exchange of money—only care.

The Way of Mindful Presence

Creator: ZenMaster Duration: 12 weeks Difficulty: Intermediate Community: 234 walkers

A progressive meditation arc that opens with breath awareness, expands into body integration, and culminates in witnessing thoughts without attachment.

Week 1 — Foundation of Breath

Daily 10-minute sits, breath counting, and an evening reflection journal.

Week 2 — Body Awareness Integration

Introduce walking meditation, mindful eating, and a 15-minute body scan.

Week 3 — Working with Thoughts

Practice thought labeling, open awareness sits, and noting techniques.

Desert Wisdom: The Path of Stillness

Creator: SandSage Duration: 8 weeks Difficulty: Advanced Community: 156 walkers

Ancient desert contemplative practices for practitioners ready to deepen into long silence, minimalism, and resilience.

Week 1 — Entering the Silence

Extended silence blocks, desert visualization journeys, and emptiness meditation.

Week 4 — Sand Labyrinth Pilgrimage

Design a personal labyrinth, walk it mindfully, and share insights with a companion.

Week 8 — Offering the Stillness

Host a quiet circle for newcomers, distilling teachings into accessible practices.

Beginner's Mind: First Steps

Creator: NewPath Duration: 4 weeks Difficulty: Beginner Community: 512 walkers

A warm welcome for those just starting out—setting up sacred space, experimenting with short practices, and building accountability buddies.

Week 1 — What is Meditation?

Learn posture basics, craft a personal altar, and set intentions with your buddy.

Week 2 — Sensing the Body

Practice body scans, introduce gentle stretching, and share weekly insights.

Week 4 — Carrying the Spark

Commit to daily micro-practices and document how mindfulness shows up in ordinary life.

How Paths Evolve

Every path is stewarded by volunteers. Once a cohort finishes, they contribute new prompts, audio guidance, or translations. Proposed future work includes a path builder, check-in reminders, and integrations with the Steward Board for mutual aid requests.

Ready to co-create a new path? Post your outline on the Steward Board so others can walk beside you.
